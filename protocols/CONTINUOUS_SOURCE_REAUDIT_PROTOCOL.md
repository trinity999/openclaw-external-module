# Continuous Source Re-Audit Protocol

## Purpose
Continuously mine EKLAVYA, ATLAS, and related sources for high-impact ideas.

## Cadence
- Light scan: weekly
- Deep re-audit: every 30 days
- Triggered re-audit: after major architecture milestone

## Rules
- Do not import blindly.
- Convert findings into Keep/Adapt/Skip triage.
- Require measurable impact hypothesis before implementation.
- Preserve integrity and cost constraints.

## Outputs
- report.md
- structured_output.json
- idea backlog entries with value/risk/effort score
